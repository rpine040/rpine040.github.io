Dental Professionals


Dr. Nolan Grayson
General Family Dentist

Dr. Grayson has is our primary dental hygienist. He has years of dental cleaning under his belt, and can refer you to one of our more specialized dentists when a more intensive treatment is required. He will help guide you down the path towards braces, dentures, and other treatments.

Dr. Alex Morrison
Orthodontist and Periodontist

Dr. Morrison deals with braces, misaligned teeth, jaw bones, and various gum related issues such as inflammation and pain. He has years of experience installing dental implants, and performing cosmetic skin grafting on gums, as well as planning and caring for custom oral hardware such as braces. He is always welcome to consultation, and will work hard to bring your smile back into shining condition.

Dr. Jessie McClaire
Prosthodontist and Endodontist

Dr. McClaire is our senior dental specialist, having worked for +20 years on replacing damaged, decayed, or missing teeth. She specializes in anything related to the inside of the tooth, and works diligently to care for any problem teeth until your bite is correct and proper.

Dental Treatments

Our facilities are supplied with the proper equipment and specialists to ensure your teeth shine bright

Our dental specialists provide a full examination of your teeth, including plaque cleaning, and a fluoride treatment. Any concerns, such as cavities, can subsequently be booked at our front desk.

Cavities are drilled and cleaned until the cavity is removed completely. The problem area is then filled with a tooth-colored resin, and corrected to ensure no change in biting pattern is developed.


One of our certified Orthodontists examines any bite problems, and will then suggest a braces treatment if necessary. Should the Orthodontist suggest braces, our plans currently start at $1,000.00. More intensive braces treatment may be subject to increased costs after consultation.


One of our certified dental specialists will examine any problems caused by loss of teeth. Should the Orthodontist suggest dentures, our plans currently start at $1,350. More intensive denture treatments may be subject to increased costs after consulation.


Our certified specialist will remove the infected inner pulp of the tooth and nerve within the root of the affected tooth. They will then clean and shape the inside of the root canal, before filling and sealing the space. A crown will then be placed on the tooth in order to protect, and restore it to its original function.
